About Kode With Klossy

Kode With Klossy is an organic creation formed by Karlie Kloss to revolutionize the future of technology starting by empowering young girls. Through providing the necessary resources and forming an inspiring environment, Kode With Klossy allows girls to grow and develop.

Commonly Asked Questions

1. What's the first day like?

Lydia: There's a lot of nervous energy but once you meet the girls and the teachers, all of it seems to melt away. Going up the elevator, I had to catch my breath because the environment and workspace was so beautiful and welcoming. It's a wonderfully crafted space.

2. What are the people like?

3. What do you learn?

Not only did we learn more about programming and programming languages but we also learned many other skills and facets. Together we built an enriching relationship with one another and we also learned effective communication skills, teamwork, leadership skills, and the importance of perservering.
